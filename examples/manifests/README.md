# examples/manifests — synthetic example manifests

Illustrative, fully synthetic manifests showing manifest **structure** (which
blocks compose a document, in what order, under what conditions). These encode no
firm drafting judgment — the firm-specific manifests, variant assembly-rules, and
citation→block linkage are private-pack content and are intentionally **not**
included in this public framework. Build your own manifests against your pack;
validate their shape with `data/schema/manifest.schema.json`.
